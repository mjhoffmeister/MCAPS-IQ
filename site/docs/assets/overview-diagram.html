  • 📖 CRM Read — opportunities, milestones, tasks, deal teams
  • 📧 M365 Read — email, Teams, calendar, transcripts
  • 📓 Vault Read — customer notes, meeting history, people
  • 📊 PBI Read — seats, ACR, consumption, pipeline metrics

🟡 Stage 2 — Review & Execute (Human-Gated)

  • 👁️ Human reviews staged changes — sees diff of what will change
  • ✏️ Human can edit progress text, task fields, next steps before applying
  • ✅ "Execute all" — applies approved changes to CRM
  • ❌ "Cancel all" — discards all staged changes, no writes
  • 🔒 Role-gated — you can only modify what MSX allows for your role
  • 📝 Execution log (.json) saved for full traceability
Read-Only Default Stage 1 is read-only against all systems. This is where 90% of interactions live.
Human-in-the-Loop Stage 2 requires explicit approval. No silent writes. No auto-execution.
Role-Gated Write permissions match your MSX role. You can only update what you'd update manually.
1 Why Plain English?

No SQL, no DAX, no OData. You describe what you need; the agent picks the right tools, queries the right systems, and assembles the answer. 43 skills activate automatically by matching your words to their capabilities.

2 Safety & Trust

Stage 1 is read-only against all systems. Stage 2 writes require explicit human approval. Role-gated: you can only modify what MSX would let you modify manually. Every staged change shows a diff.

3 Multi-Signal Intelligence

Most MSX answers come from one system. MCAPS IQ cross-references 2+ sources (CRM + M365, CRM + Vault, PBI + CRM) and flags when sources disagree or go silent.

4 Your Knowledge Compounds

The Obsidian vault stores meeting notes, customer context, and relationship history locally. Every meeting prep and action item feeds back — so the agent gets smarter about your accounts over time.
